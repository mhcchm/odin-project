Fried Chicken

source

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken thighs
  • oil
  • 50 g of paprika
  • 50 g of pepper
  • 2 garlic granules

Steps

  1. season the chicken thighs with paprika, pepper and garlic
  2. fill a frying pan with 50 ml of oil
  3. fry each chicken piece for 2 minutes
